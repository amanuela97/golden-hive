## For Sellers

### How do I become a seller on this platform?

1. Click on the "Register" button in the navigation menu
2. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
3. Select "Seller" as your role
4. Complete the email verification process
5. Complete the onboarding process by selecting "Seller" role
6. You'll be redirected to your seller dashboard

### What documentation do I need to provide as a seller?

Depending on the product category you want to sell, you may need to provide specific documentation. Common requirements include:

- Business license
- Product certifications
- Quality assurance documents
- Origin certificates
- Other category-specific documents

You can upload required documents in your seller dashboard under the "Documentation" section. All documents are reviewed by our admin team before approval.

### How do I create a product listing?

1. Log in to your seller dashboard
2. Navigate to "Products" in the dashboard menu
3. Click "Add New Product"
4. Fill in the product details:
   - Product name
   - Description
   - Price
   - Category
   - Product images
   - Tags
   - Market type (Local/International)
   - Harvest date (if applicable)
   - Origin village (if applicable)
5. Click "Create Product" to submit

**Note:** If your category requires documentation, make sure all required documents are approved before creating listings in that category.

### How do I edit or delete a product?

1. Go to your seller dashboard
2. Navigate to "Products"
3. Find the product you want to edit or delete
4. Click "Edit" to modify product details
5. Click "Delete" to remove the product (this action cannot be undone)

### What happens if my documentation is rejected?

If your documentation is rejected:

1. You'll receive a notification about the rejection
2. Review the rejection reason in your documentation dashboard
3. Update your documents and resubmit them
4. Wait for admin review and approval

You cannot create product listings in categories that require documentation until your documents are approved.

### How do I manage my product categories?

Categories are managed by administrators. If you need a new category added or have questions about existing categories, please contact support through the feedback form.

### Can I sell products in multiple categories?

Yes, as long as you have the required documentation for each category that requires it.

### How do I update my seller profile information?

1. Log in to your seller dashboard
2. Click on "Settings" (gear icon)
3. Navigate to the "Profile" tab
4. Update your information
5. Click "Save"

### How do I set up shipping and billing information?

1. Go to your dashboard settings
2. Navigate to the "Shipping" tab
3. Enter your shipping and billing addresses
4. This information will be used for order processing
5. Click "Save" to store your information

## For Customers

### How do I create an account?

1. Click on "Register" in the navigation menu
2. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
3. Select "Customer" as your role
4. Complete the email verification process
5. Complete the onboarding process
6. Start browsing and shopping!

### How do I browse products?

1. Click on "Products" in the navigation menu
2. Use the filters on the right side to narrow down your search:
   - Search by name
   - Filter by category
   - Filter by price range
   - Filter by market type (Local/International)
   - Filter by featured products
3. Sort products by price, name, or default sorting
4. Click on any product to view detailed information

### How do I add products to my cart?

**From the products page:**

- Hover over a product card and click the "Add to Cart" button that appears

**From the product detail page:**

1. Select the quantity you want
2. Click the "Add to Cart" button
3. You'll see a confirmation message

### How do I view and manage my cart?

1. Click on the cart icon in the navigation bar (shows item count)
2. View all items in your cart
3. Adjust quantities using the +/- buttons
4. Remove items by clicking the X button
5. View your cart total

**Note:** Cart items are saved to your browser's local storage, so they'll persist even if you close the browser.

### How do I checkout?

1. Add items to your cart
2. Click on the cart icon or navigate to the cart page
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. Fill in your billing information:
   - Name, email, phone
   - Address details
   - Country, city, state, ZIP code
5. (Optional) Check "Ship to a different address" if needed
6. Review your order summary
7. Select payment method (currently Cash on Delivery)
8. Add any order notes if needed
9. Click "Place Order"

**Note:** If you're logged in, your saved shipping/billing information will be automatically filled in.

### Do I need to create an account to make a purchase?

No, you can browse products and add items to your cart without an account. However, you'll need to provide your information during checkout. Creating an account allows you to:

- Save your shipping/billing information
- Track your orders (future feature)
- Receive order confirmations
- Access your order history (future feature)

### How do I search for specific products?

1. Go to the Products page
2. Use the search box in the filters section
3. Type the product name or keywords
4. Results will update automatically

### What payment methods are accepted?

Currently, we accept **Cash on Delivery (COD)**. You'll pay for your order when it's delivered to your address.

### How do I track my order?

Order tracking is a feature that will be available soon. You'll receive an email confirmation with your order details after placing an order.

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the Login page
2. Click "Forgot your password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link and enter your new password

### How do I change my password?

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings
3. Navigate to the "Security" tab
4. Enter your current password and new password
5. Click "Change Password"

### How do I update my profile information?

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings
3. Navigate to the "Profile" tab
4. Update your information
5. Click "Save"

### How do I delete my account?

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings
3. Navigate to the "Account" tab
4. Click "Delete Account"
5. Confirm the deletion

**Warning:** Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including product listings (for sellers) and order history, will be permanently deleted.

### What happens to my data if I delete my account?

- All personal information is permanently deleted
- Product listings (for sellers) are removed
- Order history is deleted
- Uploaded documents are removed
- Cart items are cleared

## Products & Listings

### How are products categorized?

Products are organized into categories to help you find what you're looking for. Categories are managed by administrators and may have specific requirements for sellers.

### What are product tags?

Tags are keywords that help describe your product. They make it easier for customers to find products through search. You can add multiple tags when creating or editing a product.

### What is the difference between "Local" and "International" market types?

- **Local:** Products available for local/domestic shipping
- **International:** Products available for international shipping

This helps customers filter products based on their shipping needs.

### What is a featured product?

Featured products are highlighted products that appear at the top of search results and may be displayed on the homepage. Featured status is managed by administrators.

### How do I report an issue with a product?

If you encounter any issues with a product listing (incorrect information, inappropriate content, etc.), please use the Feedback form to report it. Our team will review and take appropriate action.

## Orders & Payments

### When will I be charged?

For Cash on Delivery orders, you'll be charged when the product is delivered to your address.

### Can I cancel my order?

Order cancellation policies may vary. Please contact support through the feedback form if you need to cancel an order. Include your order number and reason for cancellation.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?

Order modifications are not available through the website. Please contact support through the feedback form with your order number and requested changes.

### What if I receive a damaged or incorrect product?

Please contact support immediately through the feedback form with:

- Your order number
- Photos of the issue
- Description of the problem

We'll work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

### Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact support through the feedback form with your order number and reason for refund request.

## Shipping & Delivery

### How do I add or update my shipping address?

**For logged-in users:**

1. Go to Settings
2. Navigate to the "Shipping" tab
3. Enter or update your shipping address
4. Click "Save"

**During checkout:**

- You can enter your shipping address directly in the checkout form
- If you're logged in, your saved address will be pre-filled

### What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs may vary. Currently, we offer **Free Shipping** on all orders. Check the checkout page for the most current shipping information.

### How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on:

- Your location
- Product availability
- Shipping method

You'll receive an estimated delivery time after placing your order. For specific delivery estimates, please contact support.

### Can I ship to a different address than my billing address?

Yes! During checkout, you can check the "Ship to a different address?" option and enter a separate shipping address.

### What countries do you ship to?

Shipping availability depends on the product and seller. Check the product's market type (Local/International) to see shipping options. For specific country availability, please contact support.

## Privacy & Security

### How is my personal information protected?

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information is:

- Encrypted during transmission
- Stored securely
- Only used for order processing and account management
- Never shared with third parties without your consent

### What information do you collect?

We collect:

- Account information (name, email, password - encrypted)
- Shipping and billing addresses
- Order information
- Product listings (for sellers)
- Documentation (for sellers, as required)
- Feedback and support communications

### How do you use my information?

Your information is used to:

- Process and fulfill orders
- Manage your account
- Communicate with you about orders and account updates
- Improve our services
- Comply with legal obligations

### Can I opt out of marketing communications?

Marketing communications are currently not sent. If this changes in the future, you'll be able to opt out through your account settings.

### How do I know my payment information is secure?

Currently, we use Cash on Delivery, so no payment information is stored online. If payment processing is added in the future, it will use industry-standard encryption and security measures.

### What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access to my account?

1. Change your password immediately
2. Check your account settings for any unauthorized changes
3. Contact support immediately through the Feedback form
4. Review your order history for any suspicious activity
